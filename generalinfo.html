General Info

Jack-O-Lantern Spectacular returns bigger and better than ever at the Minnesota Zoo! Gather your family and friends and come explore an outdoor trail glowing with thousands of artistically carved pumpkins. This year's event will inspire your Wanderlust with pumpkins depicting the wonders of the world and the joys of travel.

By joining us at Jack-O-Lantern Spectacular, you're supporting the Minnesota Zoo and our mission to connect people, animals, and the natural world to save wildlife.
Thank you!

Accessibility

Jack-0-Lantern Spectacular is an outdoor, nighttime event that runs rain or shine; please dress for the weather. There are special effects such as music, colorful lights, and fog throughout the experience. From Celebration Plaza, it is about a 10-minute walk (1/4 mile) on varying terrain to the start of the Jack-O-Lantern Spectacular pumpkin trail. The pumpkin trail is is an additional 1/4-mile long which takes about 40 - 50 minutes to travel. Wait time to enter may vary depending on attendance. The Jack-O-Lantern Spectacular trail is accessible to guests using mobility assistive devices , but there are elevation changes and a considerable incline along the path. Mobility devices — standard wheelchairs, extended-size wheelchairs, and motorized ECVs —are available to rent on a first-come basis at Guest Services Desk.
Strollers and wagons are welcome.

Frequently Asked Questions

In your pre-arrival email, we’ll confirm what time the gates open (either 5 or 6 p.m. depending on date you selected). You are welcome to arrive before your timeslot to enjoy dinner and activities in Central Plaza and Discovery Bay.

After you’ve enjoyed the pumpkin trail, you’re welcome to stay until the event closes (10 p.m. – 12 a.m. depending on date selected). Food, beverages, and activities are available until event closes each night.

A variety of seasonal treats and drinks are available for purchase. In Central Plaza, you’ll find dinner options, snacks, sweets and beverages. On the way to and along the pumpkin trail, you’ll find more snack and beverage stands. There are vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, and dairy-free options.

Follow us on social media @mnzoo for a preview of the seasonal treats available only at Jack-O-Lantern Spectacular.

No outside food and drink are allowed at Jack-O-Lantern Spectacular. You can bring water and baby bottles.

The Free to Explore Program does not apply to Jack-O-Lantern Spectacular. This program is available for daytime Zoo admission only.

This seasonal family friendly event aims to delight and amaze people of all ages. Some of the pumpkin designs feature creepy crawly animals--but never anything scary or haunted. Please note that the entire Jack-O-Lantern Spectacular experience is outdoors and involves considerable walking. Guests should dress for the weather conditions and wear comfortable footwear. Tickets must be reserved for all, including those under age 2.

If you want to exchange your tickets for another available day/time, a ticket change fee of $10/transaction will apply. To change your tickets, call the Minnesota Zoo Box Office during normal Zoo hours at 952-431-9200, no later than the day before your scheduled Jack-O-Lantern Spectacular visit.

Tickets are nonrefundable.

Jack-O-Lantern Spectacular is open rain or shine. In the event of heavy rain, high winds, or other inclement weather, we may cancel the event. Ticket holders will be contacted by Zoo Guest Services to be reimbursed or reschedule when possible.

We do not allow flash photography, tripods, monopods, or selfie sticks as a courtesy and safety measure. Otherwise, you may take photos and videos as long as it does not impede the views of others or the flow of traffic on the trail.

You can view animals in Discovery Bay until 8 p.m. All other animal viewing areas and the Treetop Trail are closed during Jack-O-Lantern Spectacular.
